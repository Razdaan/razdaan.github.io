About Razdaan

Philosopher, educator, cautious optimist.

Razdaan works at the intersections of postmodern critique, urban design, and pedagogies of care. Essays, monologues, and notebooks translate dense theoretical questions into lived, communal practice.

Each piece is annotated, cross-referenced, and linked to teaching resources. If you are an educator, organizer, or fellow traveler, reach out—the work flourishes in company.
