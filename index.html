About Razdaan

Philosopher, educator, cautious optimist.

Razdaan is a philosopher working at the intersections of postmodern critique, urban design, and pedagogies of care. This space collects essays, dialogues, and experiments that attempt to translate dense theoretical questions into lived, communal practice.

Each piece is annotated, cross-referenced, and linked to teaching resources. If you are an educator, organizer, or fellow traveler, reach out for collaboration: the work flourishes in company.
